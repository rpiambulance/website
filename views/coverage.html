Request Coverage

RPI Ambulance provides standby coverage for on-campus events and functions in the nearby area.
We can cover everything from a single-EMT standby to a large-scale event with multiple medical crews and patient care facilities.

To request coverage, send us an email at officers@rpiambulance.com
Be sure to include information including a point of contact, the event dates/times, event location, and estimated attendance.


  • Tier I

    $10/hour*
  • Long duration; low capacity
  • No dedicated patient care facility
  • At least 1 EMT
  • First Response 59
  • Oxygen and medical supplies

  • Tier II

    $50/hour*
  • Larger spectator event
  • No dedicated patient care facility
  • 1–3 EMTs on field crews
  • 5939 (remains available for 911 calls)
  • Oxygen and medical supplies

  • Tier III

    $150/hour*
  • Large spectator events (5,000+)
  • Dedicated patient care facility
  • 3–5 EMTs on field crews
  • 5939 (dedicated standby)
  • 2+ emergency event supervisors


Please note: A minimum of 2 weeks notice is required for event standbys.

* Most events within the RPI Community will be staffed free of charge.
