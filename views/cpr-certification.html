CPR Certification

RPI Ambulance typically holds CPR and First Aid classes several times a semester. Certification is provided by the American Heart Association and is applicable for community members, Lifeguards, EMS personnel, etc. Any RPI Community member may take part of one of our CPR or First Aid classes.

If you interested in attending or receiving more information about CPR training - contact us or reach out to cpr@rpiambulance.com
